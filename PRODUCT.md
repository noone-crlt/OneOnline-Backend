# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Nguoi doc tre va nguoi di lam thuong doc vao buoi toi, tranh thu nhung khoang thoi gian ngan de tim sach, doc tiep chuong dang do, hoac lang nghe audiobook khi nghi ngoi. Ho can mot khong gian online de kham pha sach de dang, duoc goi y co gu, va co cam giac yen tinh thay vi ap luc mua sam.

## Product Purpose

Book Area la trang web doc sach truc tuyen ket hop sach dien tu, audiobook va kham pha tac gia. Muc tieu cua san pham la bien viec tim va bat dau doc mot cuon sach tro nen mem, nhanh, va co cam xuc, dong thoi giup nguoi dung quay lai deu dan de tiep tuc hanh trinh doc cua ho.

## Brand Personality

Am, tinh te, sau lang. Giong dieu nen goi mo nhu mot thu vien mo den khuya: gan gui, co chon loc, khong khoa truong. Giao dien can tao cam giac duoc dan loi boi mot nguoi gioi sach co gu hon la mot san thuong mai dang ep chuyen doi.

## Anti-references

Khong giong homepage SaaS voi hero xanh tim, metric cards va icon grid lap lai. Khong giong san thuong mai giam gia la liet, nhieu badge do, va cac khoi san pham dong deu nhu marketplace. Khong theo huong toi tan, lanh, cyber, hoac glassmorphism trang tri.

## Design Principles

1. Dat tam trang doc len truoc hanh vi mua.
2. Goi mo su chon loc bang nhiep dieu, anh, va typography thay vi nhieu card.
3. Bien hanh trinh kham pha sach thanh mot dong chay mem, co section ro rang va co diem dung thi giac.
4. Ton vinh noi dung va tac gia, khong de UI ap dao boi cac thu phu tro.
5. Giup nguoi dung bat dau ngay, nhung van de lai du am de muon quay lai.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Huong toi WCAG AA cho do tuong phan, focus state va keyboard navigation. Ton trong reduced motion bang cach gioi han animation vao opacity va transform, tranh cac chuyen dong giat manh. Van ban can de doc tren desktop va mobile, voi cap chieu dai dong hop ly cho noi dung dai.
